About Me

With over a decade of experience in data science and bioinformatics at top institutions worldwide, I've worked on everything from cancer genomics to digital health solutions. What drives me is solving complex puzzles through data - whether that's developing AI-powered medical diagnosis tools or unraveling the mysteries of biological systems. Currently, I'm helping to revolutionize healthcare through innovative machine learning approaches at Xund in Vienna.

Research

My current position, as a Senior Data Scientist at Xund involves applying machine learning, natural language processing, and classical statistics to medical data. I lead the transition to scalable ML frameworks for improved product development and work on the certification of medical products through evaluation, documentation, and stakeholder alignment.
